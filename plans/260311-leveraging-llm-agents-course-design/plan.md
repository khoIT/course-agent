# Course Design Plan — Sử dụng Skills để khai thác sức mạnh LLM Agent

## Overview

Design and produce a complete 5-session (5×60min) online course in Vietnamese for non-technical business users. Full package: slides, facilitator guide, learner handout, activities, assessments, evaluation.

## Target Learner Summary

- Business users (office workers, managers, marketing/HR/ops)
- Mix of casual AI users + prompt-familiar users
- Visual + Kinesthetic learning styles preferred
- Online delivery (Zoom/Teams)
