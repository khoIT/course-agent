## Key Research Findings (Summary)

**7 Core Topics (Teaching Order):**
1. LLM fundamentals (what they are, how they work, mental models)
2. Chat-based AI usage (Claude.ai, ChatGPT limitations)
3. Why agentic AI (Claude Code) is better than chat
4. Prompt engineering principles (clarity, specificity, role-setting, few-shot, chain-of-thought)
5. Context engineering (smart zone, RPI workflow, compaction)
6. AI agents overview (tools, MCP, Model Context Protocol)
7. Skills & custom workflows (design, iteration, governance)

**Key Insights:**
- **MCP adoption:** 100K → 8M downloads in 5 months; becoming industry standard
- **Agent ROI:** 60–85% time reduction; 70–95% error reduction
- **Context engineering:** Smart zone (40–60% utilization) = critical mindset for long projects
- **Skills accessibility:** Business users can design workflows without coding (drag-drop + natural language)
- **Security gap:** MCP has known risks; course should include mitigation patterns

**Recommended Session Structure:** 6 sessions × 1.5–2 hours = 10 hours
- **40% Conceptual** (LLMs, agents, context engineering, MCP)
- **50% Hands-on** (prompt iteration, agent use, skill building)
- **10% Governance** (security, versioning, audit)
