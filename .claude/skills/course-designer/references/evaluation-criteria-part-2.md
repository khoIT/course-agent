# Course Design Quality Evaluation Criteria (Part 2: Criteria 11-19)

> Rating scale: P (Pass) | NI (Needs Improvement) | NA (Not Addressed)

## V. Content Slides (Criteria 11-13)

### Criterion 11: Slide Master Created
- Uses consistent Slide Master template
- Visual consistency across all slides
- **Pass**: Slide Master properly set up with consistent branding/layout
- **NI**: Inconsistent slide formatting
- **NA**: No Slide Master used

### Criterion 12: Design Principles Applied
Four principles:
1. **Easy to Read** (KISS: Keep It Short and Simple)
2. **Easy to Understand** (meaningful images replace text)
3. **Easy to Remember** (highlight key content with large fonts, bold colors)
4. **Dynamic** (warm+cool color contrast, ~30% warm, consistent color usage)
- **Pass**: All four principles clearly demonstrated
- **NI**: Some principles applied but not consistently
- **NA**: Text-heavy slides with no design consideration

### Criterion 13: Dynamic and Engaging Slides
- Illustrative images and visuals
- SmartArt for diagrams and concepts
- Presentation effects/animations
- One key message per slide
- **Pass**: Visually rich, dynamic, and engaging
- **NI**: Some visual elements but mostly text-based
- **NA**: Plain text slides with no visual enhancement

## VI. Learning Activities (Criteria 14-18)

### Criterion 14: Clear Activity Objectives
- Each activity has a clear learning objective
- Objectives tie directly to course content
- **Pass**: Every activity has explicit objectives linked to content
- **NI**: Some activities lack clear objectives
- **NA**: Activities have no stated objectives

### Criterion 15: Activities Meet REAL Criteria
- **R**elevant: Directly relates to content and learner context
- **E**ngaging: Captures interest and participation
- **A**ctive: Learners doing, not just watching
- **L**earner-centered: Builds on learner experience
- **Pass**: All four REAL criteria clearly met
- **NI**: Some but not all REAL criteria met
- **NA**: Activities are passive or irrelevant

### Criterion 16: Detailed Activity Procedures
- Step-by-step instructions
- Clear facilitator and learner actions at each step
- Timing per step specified
- **Pass**: Detailed enough for any facilitator to execute
- **NI**: Too vague or too brief
- **NA**: No procedures provided

### Criterion 17: Complete Activity Materials
- All needed materials designed and prepared
- Worksheets, case studies, role-play scenarios, game materials
- Self-explanatory and ready to use
- **Pass**: All materials complete, well-designed, ready for use
- **NI**: Some materials missing or incomplete
- **NA**: No materials prepared

### Criterion 18: Activity Debrief Connects to Content
- Structured debrief/wrap-up for each activity
- Explicitly connects outcomes back to course content
- Key takeaways summarized
- **Pass**: Every activity has debrief bridging to content
- **NI**: Debriefs exist but don't clearly connect
- **NA**: No activity debriefs

## VII. Accessibility & Inclusion (Criterion 19)

### Criterion 19: Accessible and Inclusive Design
- Sufficient color contrast (WCAG AA: 4.5:1 text, 3:1 large text)
- Meaning never conveyed by color alone
- Multi-modal activity alternatives
- Inclusive language, jargon explained
- Screen-reader-friendly (heading hierarchy, alt-text guidance)
- Considers: language proficiency, physical abilities, neurodiversity
- **Pass**: Conscious accessibility choices across all materials
- **NI**: Some accessibility considered but gaps remain
- **NA**: No accessibility considerations
