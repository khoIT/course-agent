# Content Sources — Curated Domains for Deep Research

Rich content domains that consistently produce actionable, framework-level knowledge. During research phases, include these domains in WebSearch queries and prioritize reading their content in full during Deep Dive.

## How to Use This File

- **During Scout (Stage 1):** Add `site:domain.com` to WebSearch queries for relevant domains
- **During Deep Dive (Stage 2):** Prioritize sources from these domains for full reading
- **Periodically:** Review and update this list as new high-quality sources are discovered
